RCG

Project information

  • Technologies: Java,Spring,Kafka,Apache camel,Microservice
  • Client: Argonaut
  • Project date: May, 2023
  • Project URL: www.royalcaribbean.com

The Royal Caribbean Group (RCG), comprising brands Royal Caribbean, Silversea, and Celebrity, is a prestigious client of iTravel Cruise. iTravel Cruise will enable RCG to evolve from selling a cruise cabin to becoming a “total vacation provider” that can deliver personalized guest services at every step of the trip. The product offers improved services that contribute to the entire vacation experience, such as booking flights, hotels, and transfers, as well as providing guests with personalized offers, credits, and loyalty rewards throughout the trip using an integrated guest wallet.
